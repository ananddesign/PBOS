Writing Playbook
Post structures · voice rules · never-do list
Observation post · Monday
1Hook — pattern or provocation
2The observation itself
3Pattern noticed across contexts
4Concrete example
5The insight / so what
6Discussion question to close
Cool Find post · Thursday
1Hook — "Found something..."
2What it is (1 line)
3Why it matters (the signal)
4Specific use case
5Who should actually care
6CTA — link or question

1Hook — the unexpected result
2What I actually tried
3Why I tried it
4The process (brief)
5What worked / what failed
6Big lesson + CTA
